Seed/Angel Investing

investment experience

Innvotec primary focus will continue to be private equity fund management, undertaken at venture capital, early stage and development capital levels. The firm partners have sustainable experience of global investment.

As well as providing funding, a broad range of support is available to investee companies, be that in the appointment of non-executive directors, mentors, sourcing of locum finance directors, strategic advice or commercial introductions. These activities serve to nurture the Innvotec portfolio.

methodology to

identify investment opportunities

The methodology employed to identify investment opportunities is pragmatic rather than formulaic. Common themes include those companies with innovative technologies that are likely to be patentable, with high barriers to entry and the prospect of significant sales. Innvotec also assists in the commercialisation of exciting research and development coming out of universities and institutions. Investment may be made at any stage from inception to mature.

Services & Support

Business Advice

Business advice via our mentorship programme and our management team. Innvotec has access to a global team of mentors that assist companies by taking non-executive board seats in multiple industry sectors.

Support in Funding Raises

Innvotec’s funds provide a source of capital for investee companies. Support in the fund raising activities of start-ups up to $5m in funding raises.

Business services

Business services through the provision of complete human resources, operations finance, legal, marketing, technology, programme delivery and development expertise.

Networking

Networking via our network of business and educational contacts, other start-ups and institutional connections.
