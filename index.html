Innvotec Advantages

Investors
Innvotec Limited is an independent fund manager, specialising in venture capital via investment funds for institutional investors and those private investors wishing to take advantage of tax-efficient investing.
Funds
Over the last decade, Innvotec has focused on identifying tax-efficient investment opportunities within the Alternative Investment Funds sector through the development of an increasingly broad range of specialist and generalist funds.

With over 30 years of experience investing in the venture capital market, Innvotec identifies and supports start-ups and early-stage private companies, generating absolute returns for those investing in its funds.
